Inclusion and Exclusion Criteria (Preliminary)

Inclusion Criteria: - peer-reviewed material - published >= 2000 - stratify MTHFR C677T variants (CC, CT, and TT) separately - measure 1+ of the relevant biomarkers: * Methionine, Homocysteine, Choline, Folate, Betaine, SAM/SAH, or Dimethylglycine - measure biomarker(s) biologically (not via dietary intake) and quantitatively and extractable form. * extractable forms include: mean±SD, median+spread, effect estimate, or figure extractable. - comparable assay methodology - if intervention study: include baseline/control genotype-stratified biomarker data only. Exclude post-intervention biomarker outcomes.

Exclusion Criteria: - intervention studies where patient comorbidities are expected to impact one-carbon metabolism - studies that meet inclusion criteria but only report post-treatment metrics - no genotype stratification - self reported biomarker intake or other proxy measures
- outcomes irrelevant to one-carbon metabolism or liver biology

Additional Criteria Notes:

Homozygote-only studies (CC vs TT without CT) may be retained for supporting evidence for directional trends, but will not be used for gradient CT inference.

Studies using human or well-characterized translational model systems (e.g., Mthfr677C>T murine models) may be eligible if they reported genotype-stratified quantitative measurements of relevant one-carbon biomarkers.
